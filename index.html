    • Research

    My work is at the crossroad of symplectic geometry and low-dimensional topology. In particular, I use methods from bordered Heegaard Floer homology and Fukaya categories to study invariants of such objects as 3-manifolds, knots, mapping classes of surfaces. Take a look at this brief introduction to my field for more context, and papers below for more details.

    • Comparing homological invariants for mapping classes of surfaces.
